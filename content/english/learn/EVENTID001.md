On **3 February 2023**, I had the honour of witnessing and being part of the creation of the **“Largest Chinese Character Made of Angbao”** at Ci Yuan Community Centre (People’s Association) as part of my **Chingay 2023 volunteering experience**. This achievement was officially recognised by the **Singapore Book of Records** and was featured on **Channel NewsAsia, Channel 8, and Channel U**.

This experience allowed me to work alongside passionate volunteers and community members, contributing directly to a large-scale collaborative effort. I was able to witness first-hand the coordination, teamwork, and effort required to bring such a meaningful community project to life.

![photo](https://media.licdn.com/dms/image/v2/C5622AQEdgT3Fc5fFfQ/feedshare-shrink_1280/feedshare-shrink_1280/0/1677427146872?e=1781740800&v=beta&t=09jP2NHGsVeDkG77glGTxq2VvPXdsjvzoPxnuLWFDns)


As someone who is passionate about volunteerism and community engagement, I strongly believe that giving back to society is an important responsibility. I hope more youths in Singapore will be inspired to take part in initiatives that create positive change within their communities.

I am grateful to have been part of this meaningful experience.
