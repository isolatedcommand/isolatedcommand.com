Organised by the **Association of Information Security Professionals (AiSP)** and supported by the **Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA)** and **Ensign InfoSecurity**, the Student Volunteer Recognition Programme (SVRP) 2020 recognises youths who have contributed to Singapore’s cybersecurity ecosystem.

I am honoured to have received the **SVRP Gold Medal** from **Dr Janil Puthucheary**, Senior Minister of State, Ministry of Communications and Information & Ministry of Health, in recognition of our collective cybersecurity contributions.

This recognition was shared with fellow recipients **Matthias Loong, Tay Gao Jun, Tabatha Chew, and Kar Wei Loh**.
