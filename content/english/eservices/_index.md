## ICCEN System (Isolated Command Central Enquiries System)

> [Access Here](iccens)

Built by **conanzhangtech**, The **Isolated Command NATO Translator** is a one-stop service that enables members of the public and partners to connect with the our Volunteers.

Powered by **Microsoft Forms** and **Power Automate**, this system streamlines enquiry submissions, automates routing, and ensures efficient tracking and response management.

You may submit enquiries related to:

- Requesting for an individual volunteer's Resume for professional opportunities;
- Volunteer deployment requests;
- Hackathon participation and collaboration;
- Project or technical collaboration proposals.

## IC NATO Translator App

> [Access Here](ICNATOTranslatorApp)

Built by **conanzhangtech**, the **Isolated Command NATO Translator App** is a web-based utility that converts text, words, and phrases into the NATO Phonetic Alphabet.

This tool helps users communicate letters clearly and accurately over voice communications, reducing misunderstandings caused by similar-sounding characters.

- Convert text into NATO Phonetic Alphabet format
- Supports words, phrases, and alphanumeric strings
- Useful for radio communications, cybersecurity operations, customer support, and aviation-related communications
- Simple and easy-to-use interface
