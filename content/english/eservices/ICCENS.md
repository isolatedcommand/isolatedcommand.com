## eServices Name

ICCEN System (Isolated Command Central Enquiries System)

---

## Our Mission
The **Isolated Command NATO Translator** is a one-stop service that enables members of the public and partners to connect with the our Volunteers.

Powered by **Microsoft Forms** and **Power Automate**, this system streamlines enquiry submissions, automates routing, and ensures efficient tracking and response management.

You may submit enquiries related to:

- Requesting for an individual volunteer's Resume for professional opportunities;
- Volunteer deployment requests;
- Hackathon participation and collaboration;
- Project or technical collaboration proposals.
