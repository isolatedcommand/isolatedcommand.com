> ### **About this Award and Achievement**
> <hr>
> 
> The ISC2 Cyber Leadership Program, delivered in collaboration with the Cyber Leadership Institute (CLI), is designed to empower cybersecurity professionals with the leadership, business, and strategic skills required to succeed in today's rapidly evolving digital landscape. 
> 
> Through expert-led sessions, practical frameworks, and peer collaboration, participants learn how to align cybersecurity initiatives with business objectives, communicate effectively with executives, and lead high-performing security teams. 
> 
> The program supports the development of future cybersecurity leaders who can drive organisational resilience and strategic growth.
> 
>> More Information: https://cyberleadershipinstitute.com/cyber-leadership-program-version-2/
