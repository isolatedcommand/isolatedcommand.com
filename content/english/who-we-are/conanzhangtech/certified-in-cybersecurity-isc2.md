> ### **Issuer**
> <hr>
> 
> International Information System Security Certification Consortium, or ISC2

> ### **About this Award and Achievement**
> <hr>
> 
> ISC2 developed the Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) credential for newcomers to the field, to recognise the growing trend of people entering the cybersecurity workforce without direct IT experience. 
>
> Getting Certified in Cybersecurity provides employers with the confidence that you have a solid grasp of the right technical concepts, and a demonstrated aptitude to learn on the job.
>
> As an ISC2 certification, those who hold the CC are backed by the world’s largest network of certified cybersecurity professionals helping them continue their professional development and earn new achievements and qualifications throughout their career.
> 
>> More Information: https://www.isc2.org/certifications/cc
