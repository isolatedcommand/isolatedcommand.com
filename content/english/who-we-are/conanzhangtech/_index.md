## **About**

Conan is a hands‑on learner who thrives in environments where real impact is made. Whether in tech projects, youth‑led initiatives, or community programmes, he is driven by curiosity, problem‑solving, and growing alongside his peers.

He works closely with like‑minded youths like himself to build skills, lead initiatives, and create meaningful change. Alongside his interest in technology and innovation, Conan dedicates significant time to volunteering — supporting events, mentoring peers, and contributing to community projects.

His work has been recognised through multiple awards and leadership opportunities. One of his latest ideas with his peer is the creation of an isolated command team.

At his core, Conan values growth, purpose, and staying open to new opportunities that strengthen the impact he can make.
